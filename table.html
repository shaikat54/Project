Information Ranges
Blood Pressure (B.P) Blood Pressure is the pressure of circulating blood on the walls of blood vessels.
  1. Low : Less than 90/60 (mm Hg)
  2. Normal : 120/80 (mm Hg)
  3. High : More than 140/80 (mm Hg)
Cholesterol Cholesterol is a waxy ,fat-like substance that's found in all cells in your body.
  1. Adults : Less than 200 (mg/dL)
  2. BorderLine High : 200-239 (mg/dL)
  3. High : More than 240 (mg/dL)
